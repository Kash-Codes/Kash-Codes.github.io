Hello, my name is Kash Ratliff

About Me

In my free time I enjoy playing video games of all sorts, but typically you'll catch me online in Halo matchmaking. I've been creating websites and applications for personal use for over 20 years, and hope that one day I can get the chance to apply the skills I've learned over the years in order to help a team grow and propel into the future. Aside from that, I enjoy making things. I've got a growing collection of 3D printers and love creating models to print. Some things I've created include wall mounts for computer screens, a headboard mount for a Nintendo Switch (Play while laying down and don't worry about holding the device. How cool is that!?), wall-mounted holders for my wife's ever expanding jewelry collection, Hinged Dice boxes for my D&D group, and many more. Speaking of D&D, I am the forever DM in my group and enjoy creating stories for us to collaborate and build upon. Our current campaign has been running since March 2021. We've had some people leave and some people join, but it's been so much fun seeing them interact with the world I've created.

My amazing wife and I live in Buda, Texas currently and enjoy spending time together gaming, watching TV, and hanging out with our 5 cats (Mjolnir, Marie, Severin (Steve), Oscar, and Charlie), 1 dog (Krypton), and 1 bird (Birt).

I haven't had much time to work on this portfolio site, but keep on the lookout for more to come in the near future!
